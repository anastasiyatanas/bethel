Who we are:

We are a young adults group (from ages 18+) for anyone who is looking to make friends or just to find somewhere to go on Friday nights. Our usual layout is, we start off with Wordhip at 7, and then we have a short word spoken, and then go into discussion about, either a book of the Bible or a topic that most people are interested in. Once a month we do some kind of ministry and have a "Random Day" where we choose something to do, outside of our normal routine, like volleyball, game night, movie night, etc. We also offer free Dazbog coffee and tea for whoever wants soe, and we offer food, as we know most people are coming from work or school.
